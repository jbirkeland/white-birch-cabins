About Us

Originally established in 1932, this beautiful lake front resort, surrounded by awe inspiring panoramic views of deep greens and blue landscapes, combined with unspoiled natural beauty of birch, spruce, maples, vibrant sunsets and calm summer breezes, is proud to be offering a truly unique hi-end boutique resort experience.

The White Birch experience is for those individuals who seek and appreciate a well-deserved taste of luxury resorts while on vacation. With a relaxed approach to luxury for guests of all ages and sizes, we are excited to offer 2600 acres of four season's recreational adventure to explore, accompanied with endless and unmatched resort amenities minus the crowds. As many guests comment, we feel as though we are in our own private custom lake home with beautiful refined style and decor.

In addition to our beautiful cabins shown above, the resort has more than 30 all-inclusive resort experiences and activities for guests to enjoy. Guests can take advantage of all forms of lake recreation, such as, fishing, kayaking, canoeing, boating and swimming in the summer to ice fishing, snowmobiling, snow shoeing and cross-country skiing in the winter. Even though we have ample amounts of entertainment available on-site, rest assured, the area also features countless access to over 90 local attraction and activities, such as golfing, hiking, biking, skiing, snow tubing, site seeing, shopping and much more. White Birch Resort is located within five miles of the surrounding tourist communities of Blackduck, Bemidji, Upper Red Lake, Park Rapids, Grand Rapids and Walker. Sometimes guests even choose White Birch Resort as their home base because accommodations and amenities are so great!
